This Privacy Policy describes how HaloBharat ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register and use our App:

  • Contact Information: Phone number, email address
  • Personal Details: Full name, gender
  • Address Information: Building/apartment number, locality, street, sector, city, state, pincode, and location coordinates (latitude/longitude) when provided
  • Authentication Data: Access tokens and refresh tokens for maintaining your session

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the App, including:

  • Products you view, search for, and purchase
  • Items added to your shopping cart and wishlist
  • Order history and transaction details
  • Project information and assignments
  • Reviews and ratings you submit
  • Support tickets and chat messages
  • Notification preferences and interactions

1.3 Device Information

We may collect information about your device, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version and usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify your identity using phone number authentication (OTP)
  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To deliver products to your specified addresses
  • To manage your shopping cart, wishlist, and projects
  • To send you order confirmations, updates, and notifications
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send you promotional offers and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with your explicit consent

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Authentication: We use Google Firebase Authentication for phone number verification and user authentication. Firebase's privacy policy applies to their services.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle your information.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is stored securely on our servers with encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.
